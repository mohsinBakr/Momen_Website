What Is Liposuction?

Liposuction is a body contouring procedure designed to remove stubborn pockets of fat that resist diet and exercise. Liposuction Procedure Details

During your consultation at our Riyadh clinic, Dr. Bakr performs a thorough assessment of your body composition and discusses the areas you wish to target. The procedure typically takes 1 to 3 hours depending on the number of treatment zones. Small incisions are made in discreet locations, and a thin cannula is used to gently suction out unwanted fat deposits.

With Vaser liposuction, ultrasound energy first liquefies the fat while preserving surrounding tissues, nerves, and blood vessels. This results in less bruising, reduced swelling, and a smoother final contour. Most patients wear a compression garment for several weeks and can return to light activities within 5 to 7 days.

Frequently Asked Questions About Liposuction in Riyadh

How much does liposuction cost in Riyadh?

Liposuction costs vary based on the number of areas treated and the technique used. Dr. Bakr provides a personalized quote during your consultation after evaluating your goals and treatment plan.

What is the recovery time after liposuction?

Most patients return to desk work within 5 to 7 days and resume exercise after 4 to 6 weeks. A compression garment is typically worn for 4 to 6 weeks to support healing and optimize results.

Are liposuction results permanent?

Yes, the fat cells removed during liposuction do not regenerate. However, maintaining a stable weight through healthy diet and exercise is essential to preserve your sculpted results long-term.

Who is a good candidate for liposuction?

Ideal candidates are adults near their target weight who have localized fat deposits that do not respond to diet or exercise. Good skin elasticity and overall health are also important factors that Dr. Bakr evaluates during your consultation.
