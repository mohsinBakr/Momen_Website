Rhinoplasty Procedure Details

During your consultation in Riyadh, Dr. Bakr will carefully evaluate your nasal structure and discuss your goals. The procedure typically takes 1.5 to 3 hours and involves reshaping the bone and cartilage to achieve the desired contour while maintaining proper breathing function.

Recovery involves some swelling and bruising for the first week, with most patients returning to daily activities within 10 to 14 days. Final results become fully apparent over several months as the nose settles into its refined shape.

Is rhinoplasty painful?

The procedure is performed under general anesthesia. Post-operative discomfort is manageable with prescribed medication, and most patients report minimal pain.

How long is the recovery from rhinoplasty?

Most patients return to work within 10–14 days. Swelling gradually subsides over several months, with final results visible after approximately one year.

Can rhinoplasty improve breathing?

Yes, functional rhinoplasty can correct structural issues like a deviated septum, improving both the appearance and breathing function of the nose.
