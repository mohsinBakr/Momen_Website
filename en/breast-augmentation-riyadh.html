What Is Breast Augmentation?

Breast augmentation is a cosmetic procedure that enhances breast size, shape, and symmetry using implants or a combination of implants and a breast lift. It is one of the most commonly requested procedures in Riyadh, helping women achieve the fullness and contour they desire. Breast Augmentation Procedure Details

During your consultation in Riyadh, Dr. Bakr will discuss your desired outcome and help you choose the right implant type and size using 3D simulation when available. The surgery is performed under general anesthesia and typically takes 1 to 2 hours. Incisions are made in discreet locations — under the breast fold, around the areola, or in the armpit — to minimize visible scarring.

Recovery generally involves wearing a supportive surgical bra for 4 to 6 weeks. Most patients return to light daily activities within one week and resume full exercise after 6 weeks. The implants settle into a soft, natural position over the following months.
