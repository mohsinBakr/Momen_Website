What Is a Mommy Makeover?

A mommy makeover is a personalized combination of cosmetic procedures designed to address the physical changes that occur after pregnancy, childbirth, and breastfeeding. Common concerns include loose abdominal skin, separated muscles, deflated or sagging breasts, and stubborn fat deposits. By combining procedures into a single surgical session, Dr. Bakr can restore your pre-pregnancy body with one recovery period instead of multiple separate surgeries.

Mommy Makeover Procedure Details

During your initial consultation in Riyadh, Dr. Bakr will evaluate the areas of concern and design a surgical plan that may include an abdominoplasty (tummy tuck) to remove excess skin and tighten abdominal muscles, a breast augmentation or lift to restore volume and shape, and liposuction to eliminate stubborn fat from the waist, hips, or thighs.

The combined procedure is performed under general anesthesia and typically lasts 3 to 5 hours depending on the extent of work. Recovery time varies, but most patients take 2 to 3 weeks off from daily responsibilities. A compression garment is worn for several weeks, and gradual return to exercise is recommended after 6 to 8 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions About Mommy Makeovers in Riyadh

What procedures are typically included in a mommy makeover?

A mommy makeover commonly includes a tummy tuck, breast augmentation or lift, and liposuction. Dr. Bakr customizes the combination based on your unique needs, and additional procedures such as thigh lift or arm lift can be included if desired.

What is the recovery time for a mommy makeover?

Most patients need 2 to 3 weeks for initial recovery and can return to light activities during that time. Full recovery, including resuming exercise, typically takes 6 to 8 weeks. Dr. Bakr provides a detailed recovery timeline during your consultation.

How long after pregnancy should I wait for a mommy makeover?

Dr. Bakr recommends waiting at least 6 months after your last pregnancy and 3 to 6 months after finishing breastfeeding. This allows your body to stabilize and ensures your weight has returned to a consistent level for optimal surgical results.

Is a mommy makeover safe?

Yes, when performed by an experienced board-certified plastic surgeon like Dr. Bakr, a mommy makeover is a safe procedure. He conducts thorough pre-operative evaluations and uses advanced surgical techniques to minimize risks and ensure a smooth recovery.
