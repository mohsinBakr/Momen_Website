What Is a Facelift?

A facelift, also known as rhytidectomy, is a surgical procedure that restores a youthful and refreshed appearance by tightening sagging skin, smoothing deep wrinkles, and redefining the jawline and neck contour. It is one of the most effective anti-aging procedures available at our Riyadh clinic.

Facelift Procedure Details

During the consultation, Dr. Bakr assesses your facial structure, skin elasticity, and aesthetic goals. The surgery typically takes 3 to 5 hours, involving carefully placed incisions hidden in the natural creases around the ears and hairline.

Recovery involves initial swelling and bruising for 1–2 weeks. Most patients return to social activities within 2–3 weeks, with results that continue to improve over several months.

How long do facelift results last?

A well-performed facelift typically lasts 7–10 years. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle and skincare routine can extend results further.

Dr. Bakr uses advanced techniques to place incisions in natural creases, making scars virtually invisible once healed.
